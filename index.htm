About The Game
 Block Skater is a logic game in which you move and smash boxes in a certain way to achieve your goal. In the game you are playing a skateboarder who is looking for his cat. There are 8 levels which increase in difficulty as you progress through the game. Your objective is to break all of the boxes in each level.

How To Play
 To win the game, break all of the boxes in each level until you find the cat. Use [k] and [l] to move around and over blocks. Press [a] to lift a box so you can move it to another location. Press [s] to break boxes. In order to lift or break a box you have to be right next to and facing towards it.

Notes
 Remember to click inside the game window before playing if it is not in focus. Some browsers may not support javascript.
